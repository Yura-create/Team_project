PETlya Veterinary Clinic:
Exceptional Care for Your Pets

Welcome to PETlya Veterinary Clinic, your trusted companion care provider. Our dedicated team of veterinarians and staff are passionate about ensuring the health and happiness of your beloved pets. From preventive care to emergency services, we offer a comprehensive range of veterinary services tailored to meet the unique needs of your furry friends. With our state-of-the-art facility and personalized approach, you can trust PETlya Veterinary Clinic to deliver exceptional care and support for your pets at every stage of their lives.

Services

Preventive Care: We emphasize the importance of regular check-ups, vaccinations, and parasite prevention to keep your pets healthy and happy.

Diagnostic Services: Our advanced diagnostic tools enable us to accurately identify and treat various health conditions. From blood tests to X-rays, we ensure a comprehensive evaluation of your pet's health.

Surgical Procedures: Our modern surgical suite is equipped to perform a wide range of procedures, including spaying/neutering, soft tissue surgeries, and orthopedic surgeries. We prioritize patient safety and utilize the latest techniques and anesthesia protocols.

Pet Boarding and Grooming: We offer a comfortable and secure boarding facility, ensuring your pet receives attentive care during your absence. Our grooming services help keep your furry companion looking and feeling their best.
